The "App-ads.txt Manager" is a Shiny web application designed to simplify the management, comparison, and analysis of app-ads.txt files, a critical component for digital advertising. App-ads.txt is a method for publishers to declare who is authorized to sell their advertising inventory, helping combat fraud and ensure transparency in the digital advertising ecosystem.
